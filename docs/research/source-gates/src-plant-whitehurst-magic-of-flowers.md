## Safety / adult-use cautions

- Many flowers are unsafe for people and domestic animals. Future extraction must identify household boundaries at item level before any flower enters direct-use review.
- Do not recommend food/drink use, essence use, topical use, smoke/fragrance practices, or baths from this source without separate safety review.
- Reject adding any flower preparation to another person's food or drink.
- Hold essential-oil, mist, diffusion, smoke, incense, bath, and body-contact practices until specific safety review.
- Do not use this source for medical, mental-health, trauma-recovery, addiction, immunity, detoxification, fertility, puberty, or diagnostic guidance.
- Avoid releasing flowers, petals, or infused water into outdoor waterways unless the environmental impact is reviewed. Compost/earth return is usually a safer adaptation.
- Keep unsafe flowers out of household runtime practice unless the final reviewed ritual uses a safe substitute, image, or purely symbolic source note.
