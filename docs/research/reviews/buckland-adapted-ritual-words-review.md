## Review posture

This review applies the post-378 operative-word contract candidate by candidate. The goal was to avoid holding candidates where the rite can remain honest through source-backed architecture, exact short source words, and functional instruction.

Review rule used:

- If the source provides usable operative wording of 20 words or fewer, preserve it exactly and track it as `source_exact_short`.
- If the source provides longer operative wording, do not invent substitute ceremonial speech.
- If the source-backed candle sequence still works through plain naming, prayer, blessing, or speech action, convert the adapted phrase to functional instruction.
- Hold only if the candidate collapses without unresolved source words.

Result: all 12 Buckland adapted-word entries were converted to functional instruction. No Buckland candidate had to be newly held.
