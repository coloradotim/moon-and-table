Do not import:

- exact Buckland ritual scripts as generated app text;
- exact poems, prayers, Psalms, full scripts, or full consecrations;
- Psalm-to-purpose mapping as app content;
- Christian/Pagan paired scripts as copied systems;
- exact diagrams copied as diagrams;
- coercive or pressure-based love/influence workings;
- jealousy, enemy, domination, or power-over-others workings;
- guaranteed health, money, prosperity, luck, success, protection, or magical-effect claims;
- dark-side appendix material;
- non-candle appendix material into this packet.

Promotion to `candidate_extract_now` is allowed when a source item can become a symbolic, non-coercive, non-guaranteed, source-backed candle ritual without medical/legal/financial/therapy claims.
