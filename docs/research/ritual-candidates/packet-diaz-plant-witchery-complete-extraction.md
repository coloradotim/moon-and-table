## Extraction Stance

This packet is source research and Ritual authoring/review only. It does not import runtime records, change eligibility, add source cards, or make any recommendation behavior active.

The source is a modern copyrighted plant-magic compendium. It contains direct rituals, spells, baths, sprays, teas, tinctures, syrups, food preparations, incense blends, plant correspondences, dream interpretations, medicinal claims, culturally specific plant-spirit language, and plant-entry guidance. This packet therefore separates:

- complete ritual-shaped source items;
- preparation-heavy or safety-heavy items that need later review;
- plant symbolism and correspondence material that is useful as source-note support but not a standalone Ritual;
- rejected or held items that would require unsafe, medicalized, coercive, culturally sensitive, or copied-source handling.

The gate is Ritual architecture and source support. Baths, ingestion, smoke, essential oils, blood/saliva, culturally sensitive materials, and target-directed workings are not silently dropped. They are inventoried and labeled for owner review.
