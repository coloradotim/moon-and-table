## Import Doctrine Applied

Moon & Table may import a self-contained, source-backed Ritual when it has a beginning/container, a middle/core action, an end/closing, intrinsic purpose, source-backed ritual logic, and enough self-containment to perform from the app. Recommendation eligibility is a separate decision. This packet therefore marks many adult or advanced candidates as findable/direct-use while holding recommendation until runtime gates can represent adult privacy, explicit body work, group play, kink, medical-adjacent claims, or high-capacity practices.
