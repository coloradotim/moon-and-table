Commit to the Practice Night

Sit together at the table with the calendar nearby. Choose one recurring window that belongs to your shared practice. Each of you names one reason the practice is worth keeping. Mark the time in the calendar, then close the calendar and leave one hand on it together for one breath.

#### Intention

Give the practice a real place in time without turning it into an obligation.

#### Best window

Before beginning a new shared ritual cycle, or when the practice has been drifting.

#### Question to carry

What recurring time can hold us without pressuring us?
