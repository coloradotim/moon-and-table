Cleaning tips, feng shui-style placement advice, plant/animal/crystal correspondences, mists, oils, colors, and recipes are preserved as support unless they have a performable ritual container.

## Import Doctrine Applied

Moon & Table imports self-contained source-backed Rituals. This source is very close to Moon & Table's domestic center: clutter clearing, space clearing, floor washes, bedrooms, hearth, home blessing, protection, and house-spirit work. Adult/safety filtering is not relevant here; the main gates are ritual architecture, performability, house voice, timing metadata, and whether the practice should be recommendation-eligible or simply findable/direct-use.

### `whitehurst-clutter-clearing-jumpstart`

- Source location: Ch. 1, pp. 24-25
- Import readiness: `approved_for_mechanical_import`
- Direct-use target: `yes`
- Recommendation target: `yes`
- Primary purpose: begin clutter clearing
- Primary carrier: white candle, energizing drink, chosen clutter area
- Audience: self
- Capacity: medium
- Review labels: clutter, clearing, candle, drink, home, medium_capacity
- Recommendation constraints: recommend for opening/clearing when capacity is at least enough to engage
- Headline: Light the First Clear Space
- Summary: A candle-and-drink ritual for beginning a manageable clutter clearing area without overwhelming the whole house.

- Intention: I begin with one place and let the clearing gather strength.
- Question: What became easier once the first place was chosen?
