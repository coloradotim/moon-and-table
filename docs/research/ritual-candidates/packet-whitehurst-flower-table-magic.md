The strongest Moon & Table shape is not `flower X means Y.` It is: a flower is placed where the household can see it; it receives a short spoken or silent purpose; it witnesses a moment; it is thanked; it is returned or preserved.

### SN-WHITEHURST-DO-NOT-PAD

The matrix is used to catch gaps, not to force every cell. Doorway/body/candlelight cells remain secondary, weak, or held unless the source gives a safe flower-first practice.
