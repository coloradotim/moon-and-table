Source grounding: Living in Moontime and Ways to Work with the Moon, PDF pp. 38-81.

Source mechanics:

- observe the current moon phase;
- note internal state without diagnosis;
- record date, phase, body/energy/mood in a grimoire;
- build pattern recognition across at least one lunation.

Moon & Table adaptation:

Beginning: open the app or household grimoire beside a candle or cup of water.

Action: note the phase, one felt condition, and one practical attention point.

Close: close with one sentence: "This is the phase I am in tonight." This short line is generated Moon & Table copy, not source text.
