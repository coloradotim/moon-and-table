## Scope and boundaries

This packet treats *The Moon Book* primarily as a lunar-cycle source: phase meaning, lunar practice rhythm, moon mapping, phase-specific ritual architecture, reflection prompts, altar/table/vessel/candle/body practices, grimoire/journal follow-up, and timing adaptation.

The packet does not convert the book into horoscope copy, medical/therapy guidance, fertility claims, guaranteed manifestation claims, or deterministic phase rules.

The source strongly supports cyclical practice. The core architecture preserved here is:

```text
choose a desire / focus
map it across a lunation
begin in quiet space
build through action
witness at fullness
release, rest, integrate
record what happened
begin again
```

Moon & Table use should stay private, warm, magical, source-backed, and non-deterministic. The Moon shapes the ritual; it does not command the user.

- disposition: `candidate_extract_now`
- ritualizationType: `source_backed_moon_and_table_form`

#### Headline
Map one desire through the Moon.

#### Ritual body / practice
At the table, write one desire or question at the center of a page. Around it, make four simple stations: beginning, building, fullness, and release. Add one action or observation for each station. Fold the page once and place it where you will see it through the cycle. Return to it at each phase and add one dated line. Close the cycle by writing what changed and what still wants another month.

#### Intention
Let the Moon give one desire a shape that can be revisited instead of rushed.

#### Best window
At or near the new Moon, or whenever beginning a month-long ritual arc.

#### Question to carry
What is this desire asking for at each part of the cycle?
