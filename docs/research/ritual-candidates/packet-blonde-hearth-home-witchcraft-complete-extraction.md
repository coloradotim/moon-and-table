# Hearth and Home Witchcraft Complete Extraction Packet

Source: Jennie Blonde, _Hearth and Home Witchcraft_  
Packet status: `ready_for_human_review_before_import`  
Extraction stance: complete mapping pass for named rituals, blessings, charms, meditations, and everyday practices. Food recipes, herbal recipes, correspondence boxes, deity profiles, altar-tool descriptions, and general advice are preserved as support unless they include a performable ritual action.

## Import Doctrine Applied

Moon & Table imports self-contained source-backed Rituals. This source is domestic, accessible, and close to the app's voice, but many entries are recipes or advice rather than Rituals. Ritual-shaped items below are approved as findable/direct-use, with recommendation eligibility separated by capacity, timing, audience, and whether the record is a recipe-support or charm-support item.
