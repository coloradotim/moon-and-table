Repairs made:

- Rewrote extraction-style `whyThisFits` fields into user-facing Moon & Table rationale.
- Removed trivial safety/manual phrasing from direct-use practice copy, including `safe object`, `safest household stand-in`, pet/trip/poison listing, and over-explained smoke/flame alternatives.
- Kept ordinary practical constraints where they are part of the rite's shape, such as using an ordinary cleaner, known plant, or surface-appropriate water.
- Kept direct-use eligibility separate from recommendation eligibility.

Headline/body fit:

- `Center the House Mind` became `Center the House`; the ritual centers household attention, not a separate entity called the house mind.
- `Sit Beside the Flame` became `Sit With the Flame`; the practice is relational and steadying, not just positional.
- `Stand at the Threshold` became `Renew the Threshold`; the body names what belongs inside and outside, so the headline now names the ritual action.
- `Doorway Guardian Object` became `Set the Doorway Guardian`; the body consecrates and places the object.
- `Key at the Door` became `Hang the Key at the Door`; the body uses a placed key as the action.
- `Bless the Table Legs` became `Bless the Four Corners of the Table`; the body blesses four table supports/corners as a whole.
- `Living Room Green Tending` became `Tend the Room Through the Plant`; the body is plant tending as room tending.
- `Four Elements House Purification` became `Carry the Four Elements Through the House`; the body is a circuit with elemental carriers.
- `Leave the Old Rooms Clear` became `Leave the Old Room Clear`; the body can apply to one room or home, but the singular headline reads more intimate.
- `Choose the Household Altar Place` became `Choose the Altar Place`; shorter and less product-like.

House-voice notes:

- The main issue was not eligibility but voice. The imported `whyThisFits` fields sounded like source extraction notes, and several headlines were labels rather than invitations into the rite.
